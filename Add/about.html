What is Add?

Add is a bilingual publication focusing on designs from all over the world, by chats and interviews about local designers/artists from different locations.

Different from mainstreams trend, add digs for unique creative beans from all over the world especially Asia. Each issue will focus on local designers and artist in a specific language or area, each issue will be in English and the focus language of the season.

Why is it call Add?

Full name is Air diverse design
